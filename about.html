The heroes, initially driven by a noble desire to liberate the oppressed, saw an opportunity amidst the misery. They feigned outrage at Valerius's tyranny, winning the trust of the desperate, broken peasants.

With honeyed words and promises of a brighter future, Zach and Jordan convinced the downtrodden to part with their meager homes. The peasants, clinging to the last embers of hope, signed away their deeds for mere pennies, their signatures blurred by tears and the constant drizzle.

The city, once a tapestry of lives, became a ghost town. Zach and Jordan, now owners of countless properties, stood amidst the empty streets with their trusty alpaca, the rain reflecting their cold, calculating eyes. They had not liberated the peasants, but stripped them bare, leaving them to wander the sodden streets, homeless and betrayed. The heroes, their initial noble intentions long forgotten, had become the very thing they claimed to fight, their names now whispered with the same fear and loathing as Lord Valerius.

Why Choose Us?

With so many home buying business out there, why choose us?

Transparency & Honesty

When it comes time to sell your house for a cash offer, we want you to choose us. And we think the best way to make that happen is to be transparent and fair from the start. That's why we'll always be open about what our cash offer is and how we arrived at it.

Reputation

Like any small business we live and die by our reviews. Throughout the whole process our aim is to earn a five star review from you just like we have dozens of others.

Speed & Flexibility

We understand that timing can be just as important a factor as money when choosing to sell your home for cash. We're dedicated to ensuring we can close every deal on your timeline.

No Hidden Fees or Costs

Did we mention that our cash offers are upfront, transparent and fair? As part of that same commitment to transparency we will never include hidden or last minute fees or costs. The offer is the offer, full stop from day one.

Professionalism & Communication

Even when choosing the easiest path of selling your home for cash, selling a home is still a big move. That's why we're committed to always being responsive and available to field your questions.

The Offer Itself

While we pride ourselves on being professional and easy to work with, we know that at the end of the day it's all about the money. That's why our first offer will always be a fair one. We'll never low-ball you.
